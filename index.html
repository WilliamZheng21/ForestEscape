Forest Escape

  • High Concept: You're a child lost in the woods trying to find your way out.
  • Player Experience Goals: I want the player to feel like they were that child and the excitement and disorientation that comes with being lost in the woods.
  • Genre: This game is a puzzle.
  • Game play: The player can move in the four cardinal directions using the arrow keys. Up, down, left, right keys to move in those respective directions.
  • Prototype goal: This prototype is evaluating tileset and the utility that they come with.
  • Features: Few custom sprites and maze design.
  • Setting/Premise: A child has found himself lost in the woods and now has to find his way out.
  • Target Audience: The target audience are young children.
  • Play time: Three to four minutes.
  • Strategies: Hug the right wall.

    Player Sprite
    Music
